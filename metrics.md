## Meeting Tips
- Be ready to discuss **quarterly cadence** for each KR — which are monthly, quarterly, or annual
- For joint goals (e.g. observability team), clarify **your contribution vs shared ownership**
- Distinguish **leading indicators** (e.g. ADRs written) from **lagging indicators** (e.g. adoption rate)
- Flag any KRs that are **dependent on headcount, budget, or other teams** early
